About me

Hello there! I'm Lacey, a 20-year-old college student with a passion for capturing moments through photography, bringing characters to life through digital sculpting, and creating animations that tell stories. As a student photographer, digital character sculptor, and animator, I pour my creativity and dedication into every project I undertake. When I'm not behind the camera or immersed in the world of digital art, you can find me cuddling with my beloved feline companions. As a devoted cat lover, these furry friends hold a special place in my heart and often inspire my creative endeavors. Join me on this artistic journey as I navigate the world of design and continue to learn and grow as a multifaceted artist. Thank you for stopping by to learn a little more about me!
