Shadow

Shadow is a beautiful grey cat with a personality as bold as his color. Sometimes he can be a bit mischievous — knocking things over or getting into places he definitely shouldn't — but it's all part of his charm. Even when he's being a little troublemaker, it's impossible to stay mad at him for long because his cuddly nature always wins you over. He's the kind of cat who will curl up right in your lap or brush against your legs, constantly asking for your attention and affection.

Lately, Shadow has been growing so fast, it's hard to believe how big he's getting. With that growth comes an even bigger appetite — he absolutely LOVES food and is never shy about letting you know when it's mealtime. Whether he's chasing after a toy, stretching out for a nap, or begging for treats, Shadow fills the house with energy, love, and a little bit of adorable chaos.

Butters

Butters is a sweet little cat with a shy soul. When you first meet her, she’s pretty skittish, keeping her distance and watching everything carefully. But with patience and time — usually a few months — she slowly opens up, revealing her playful, sneaky side. Even though she’s not as openly affectionate as Shadow, Butters has her own way of showing love, choosing very carefully when she's ready for cuddles. It’s all on her terms, but honestly, that just makes every moment she seeks out affection feel even more special.

Unlike her bigger brother Shadow, Butters is tiny and delicate, and we kind of hope she stays that way forever. She’s easy to pick up and hold, almost like carrying around a little ball of softness. Her small size just adds to her charm, and even though she might be a little more independent, she’s a huge part of the heart and soul of our home.
